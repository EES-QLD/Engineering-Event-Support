The concept

What if the trip was part of the business?

Most people take holidays and pay for them twice — once with money, and again by returning to a backlog of work. Kulhavn does it differently.

Kulhavn is built on a simple idea: combine genuine engineering experience, infrastructure curiosity, remote field work and content creation into a commercial platform that can fund travel, build audience value and create long-term business assets.

Travel becomes part of the operating model. Gear becomes business equipment. Content becomes a compounding asset. Engineering credibility creates trust. The structure protects the long-term wealth being built underneath it.

This is not a tax dodge or a lifestyle excuse. It is what happens when real work, technical curiosity and commercial travel are structured properly — with proper advisors, genuine income-producing activity and commercial substance behind every decision.

The structure

Built properly. Built to last.

The difference between a holiday and a business trip is not the destination. It is the commercial purpose, documentation, revenue model and structure behind it. Kulhavn is designed as a properly advised operating brand for engineering-led travel, infrastructure media, partnerships and expedition activity.

The goal is not to make holidays deductible. The goal is to build a business where travel, field work, media production and commercial relationships genuinely belong together — with accountants and advisors involved from the start.

Risk separation
Content, travel, vehicles, sponsorships and media activity sit separately from EES QLD engineering certification and PI risk. Properly advised and documented.
Tax-aware distributions
A family trust structure allows profits to flow efficiently across entities and beneficiaries — legally, with full accountant oversight and genuine commercial substance.
Legitimate expense offsetting
Travel, accommodation, equipment, vehicles and production costs are only claimed where there is genuine commercial connection to Kulhavn's income-producing activity.
Wealth accumulation
Profits flow into the broader Mitchell entity structure — separating operating activity from long-term investment growth and protecting the asset base being built.

The setup

The rig. The bike. The trail.

Every Kulhavn Australia route runs on the same proven setup. The 79 Series carries camp, kit and the E-MTB. The DR650 scouts the country roads, dirt tracks and fuel stops the 79 can't or shouldn't do. The E-MTB handles the singletrack. Relive captures every ride. The content documents all of it.

01
79 Series + Travelander
LandCruiser 79 Series with Travelander camper. The mothership. Carries everything — camp, E-MTB, tools, camera gear, recovery kit and enough range to go places that don't have options. Set up for extended touring across regional Australia.
02
Suzuki DR650
The scout. Light, simple, fixable anywhere and capable on dirt and bitumen equally. Handles the back roads, station tracks and fuel runs that the 79 sits out. Tracked on Relive. Proper adventure bike — not a fashion item.
03
E-MTB
For the trails and singletrack the DR650 and 79 Series can't reach. Carried on the Travelander. Every ride tracked on Relive and published. Tasmania alpine trails, Queensland high country and wherever the trail network is worth the detour.
