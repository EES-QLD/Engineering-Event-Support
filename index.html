The concept

What if the trip paid for itself?

Most people take holidays and pay for them twice — once with money, and again coming home to a pile of work. We do it differently.

Engineering Event Support is built on a simple idea: combine genuine professional engineering services with a content brand, structure it properly inside a tax-advantaged business vehicle, and let the operation fund the lifestyle.

Travel becomes an operational expense. Gear becomes equipment. Content becomes an asset that generates income while you sleep. The whole thing sits inside a business structure designed to protect wealth, distribute profit efficiently, and scale over time.

This isn't a tax dodge. It's what happens when real work naturally requires real travel — and you're smart enough to build the right infrastructure around it.

The structure

Built to save tax.
Built to last.

The difference between a holiday and a business trip isn't the destination — it's what you build around it. Engineering Event Support is structured from the ground up to make travel a legitimate, ATO-defensible business activity. Deductions, distributions, and long-term asset accumulation are built into the operating model from day one.

We work with professional accountants and advisors to ensure every element of the structure has commercial substance, revenue intent, and proper documentation. The goal isn't to make holidays deductible. The goal is to build a business where travel is genuinely necessary — and it is.

Risk separation
Content, vehicles, travel, and lifestyle operations sit in a dedicated entity — completely separated from core engineering PI exposure and certification work.
Tax-efficient distributions
A family trust structure allows profits to be distributed across multiple beneficiaries and entities — putting income where it's taxed least, legally, with full advisor oversight.
Legitimate expense offsetting
Travel, accommodation, equipment, vehicles, and content production costs offset business income — reducing taxable profit while the operation genuinely scales.
Wealth accumulation
Retained earnings flow into an investment entity for long-term accumulation — separating operational income from investment growth and protecting the asset base.
